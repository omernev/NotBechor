In the directory "People", open the file with your name.

Next to every restaurant, write a score from 1 to 6.
1 - I will never eat there.
2 - I don't really want to, but ok if we didn't eat there recently.
3 - Meh.
4 - I like eating there.
5 - I really like eating there.
6 - This is great, I want to eat there as much as possible.

You can update the file any time you want.
